For Referring Physicians

A clinical partner for post-operative care.

Refer your patients to a Registered Nurse who understands surgical protocol — and treats lymphatic recovery with the rigor it deserves.

The Case for Referral

Specialized recovery, aligned with your standards.

Your surgical results don't end in the operating room — they're shaped by the weeks that follow. Entrusting post-operative Manual Lymphatic Drainage to a Registered Nurse means your patients receive care from someone trained to recognize complications, respect contraindications, and coordinate within a medical framework.

Clinical Value

Why surgeons refer to Chastity.

RN credentials

Care delivered by a licensed Registered Nurse with clinical training and an understanding of surgical recovery.

Reduced complications

Vigilant assessment and proper technique help identify concerns early and support a smoother recovery course.

Optimized outcomes

Consistent lymphatic support helps protect the refinement and quality of your surgical result.

A Coordinated Approach

Care that respects your protocol.

Chastity works as an extension of your post-operative plan — never around it. Treatment timing, technique, and progression are guided by your instructions and the patient's individual recovery markers, with communication kept clear and professional.

  • Adheres to surgeon-directed timelines and clearance.
  • Awareness of contraindications and warning signs.
  • Professional, discreet communication with your practice.
