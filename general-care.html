Concierge Services

General Concierge Nursing.

Restorative, RN-led care and Manual Lymphatic Drainage for everyday wellness — delivered with clinical precision and a calming, personalized touch.

Overview

Baseline care, elevated.

General concierge nursing is the foundation of the practice — restorative Manual Lymphatic Drainage and wellness support for clients who are not in a surgical recovery window but want the benefits of expert lymphatic care.

Sessions are unhurried and individualized. As a Registered Nurse, Chastity brings a clinical eye to every visit, assessing how your body is responding and adjusting technique to support circulation, comfort, and overall wellbeing.

What it supports

The benefits of restorative MLD.

Reduced fluid retention

Gentle, directional technique encourages the body to clear excess fluid and ease the feeling of heaviness or swelling.

Improved circulation

Supports healthy lymph and blood flow, helping deliver the conditions your body needs to feel restored.

Whole-body wellness

A calming, restorative experience that supports immune function and a renewed sense of balance.

Is it right for you?

Thoughtful care, clinically guided.

General concierge nursing is well suited to those seeking ongoing lymphatic support, relief from everyday swelling and fatigue, or a restorative wellness routine guided by a medical professional.

Every engagement begins with a consultation to understand your history and goals, and to confirm that care is appropriate and safe for you.
